About Me

I'm a student at Grinnell College graduating in May 2020 with a degree in computer science and music. I'm also a professional musician who plays saxophone and piano. I'm currently looking for employment in software development and technology support roles.

On campus, I run a student group that facilitates student music and puts together huge music shows every semester. I enjoy playing in latin, jazz, and funk ensembles, and I host a jazz-themed radio show every week. I also have worked at the college's IT department for the past four years, where I've provided technical support to over a thousand members of the Grinnell College community.

I am very passionate about projects that tie my two disciplines, technology and music. At the moment, I hack on my own personal projects.
